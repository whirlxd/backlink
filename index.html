backlink identifier

hunts the internet for possible links based on a given backlink.

  • What backlinks does it support?

    Imgur, Youtube, Tinyurl, Goo.gl, Bit.ly, Pastebin, Mediafire, Mega, Gyazo, Dailymotion, Vimeo, Onion Links, Facebook, Reddit, Lightshot, Dropbox, Discord, IMDB, Pinterest, WhatsApp, Google Drive, Google Slides, Google forms, Google docs, Vocaroo, Clyp, Instagram, ISBN, Notion, Google Meet, Imgflip, Spotify, ControlC, Wheel of Names, ctxt.io, Imgpile
